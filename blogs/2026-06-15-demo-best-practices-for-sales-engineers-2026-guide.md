This guide gives sales engineers, solutions consultants, and presales professionals concrete strategies for delivering demos that advance deals. It emphasizes running discovery before demos, structuring presentations around buyer pain points rather than features, using stable demo environments, and following up with interactive leave-behinds for buying committees averaging 6-10 decision-makers.
